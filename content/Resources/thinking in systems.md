### Identity Principle
> «*Just because your first solution isn't perfect, doesn't mean that there's no solution*»

> «*The discomfort of making the change acting on solutions is often  not as bad as the discomfort of not making the change*»

> «If you don't make the change, you live with the stress, pressure, anxiety and disappointment of never making real progress on what matters to you»
