### TL;DR  
Lean and functional structure for a remote teamwork

---
### Key Ideas
- Each project has its own Slack channel: no work-related direct messages, only public channels;
- Shared Kanban board: the whole team view what's moving;
- Run sprints with clear goals: ever ymorning a bot checks from everyone: 
	- What did you do yesterday? 
	- What's planned today?
	- Any blockers?

---
### Identity Principle
> "No one’s waiting and no one’s guessing. And no one has to sit through meetings just to be seen"

---
### Action Idea
- Structure is a non-negotiable for a remote team
