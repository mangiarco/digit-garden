### Key Ideas
- - Merge **time boxing** and **task batching** with awareness of **energy levels**.
- Schedule high-focus, cognitively demanding tasks when energy is highest; reserve low-effort tasks for lower-energy periods.
- Helps reduce stress and increases awareness of external demands on time.

---
### Identity Principle
> «Your productivity is shaped not just by tasks or schedules, but by aligning work with your natural energy rhythms»

---
### Action Idea
- Map your weekly and daily energy peaks and troughs.
- Batch similar tasks together and assign them to time blocks that match your energy levels.
- Prioritize high-brain-power tasks for early in the day/week; low-effort tasks for later periods.
