### TL;DR

You’re not depressed, you’re just in a shit situation. List of 7 simple techniques to overcome it.

---
### Key Ideas
  
#### **1. Complete Your Ikigai**: 
Once you find your life's purpose, it's nearly impossible to feel depressed. Lifelong fulfillment is hidden in the intersection of these. Start by asking yourself what: 
	1. You love to do  
	2. You're good at  
	3. The world needs  
	4. You can be paid for  

#### **2. Dopamine Detox**: 
Freeing yourself from short-term pleasure cleanses the mind. Remove the cheap dopamine anchor and embrace the serene seas of self. I do dopamine detoxes:  
	1. For 2-3 hours every Saturday  
	2. For 2-3 days every few months

#### 3. **No Alcohol**: 
Sobriety has given me everything I sought from alcohol. Alcohol is merely a mask for your real problems.  Longer you wear it, the more you forget who you actually are.  So:  
	1. Better gut health  
	2. Retained emotional stability  
	3. Improved sleep and fitness  

#### 4. **Healthy Body = Healthy Mind** 
Symbiotic Success says that as you take better care of yourself, your outer world improves. No one does these three things regularly and is depressed. At a minimum:  
	1. Eat well  
	2. Meditate  
	3. Lift weights  

#### 5. **Spend Time in Nature**: 
Nature is my biggest mentor and therapist. Getting outside daily, taking my shoes off, and grounding myself bring infinite stability to my life. In countries like Japan, nature is even recognized as a form of clinical therapy. Embrace Earth's medicine.  
  
#### 6. Remove Negative People: 
Identify negative people by asking - after meeting someone, do I:  
	1. Feel drained?  
	2. Feel full of energy?  
	The 2nd category doesn't belong in your life.  Negativity is contagious - avoid it like you would a disease.  
  
#### **7. Never Skip 2 Days**: 
With whatever habit you're forming, only skip a maximum of one day.  If you skip two in a row? You've lost momentum.  Because, let's be real, no one's perfect. You will miss days. (linked to [[Two-days rule]])
